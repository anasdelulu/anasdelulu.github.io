About

The Malta College of Arts, Science and Technology (MCAST) is Malta’s leading vocational education and training institution, offering a wide range of practical and academic programmes designed to prepare students for real-world careers. MCAST provides education from Foundation Level up to Master’s Degree level, combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience. This approach ensures that students develop both creative thinking and technical skills that are directly relevant to industry standards. Through its various institutes, MCAST collaborates closely with local and international industries, allowing students to gain valuable experience through projects, placements, and applied learning. This strong connection with industry helps students transition smoothly from education into professional environments. The Institute for the Creative Arts (ICA), based in Mosta, focuses on disciplines such as Graphic Design, Illustration, Photography, Interactive Media, Fine Art, and Visual Communication. Students are encouraged to explore creativity, experiment with ideas, and build strong portfolios that reflect both personal identity and professional practice. Overall, MCAST aims to empower students with skills, confidence, and adaptability, preparing them to contribute effectively to the creative industries and the wider professional world.
